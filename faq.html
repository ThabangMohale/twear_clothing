WHAT WILL THE CUSTOMER SERVICE BE LIKE?

Customer service is a key focus for the business. We will do everything within our control to make the transition as smooth as possible for you, while maintaining our customer service levels. twear.co.za's business is built on the simple principle that the customer comes first, and this will not change.

HOW DO I REGISTER WITH TWEAR?

To register as a user, click here or on Register at the top of the Twear website and create an account. We’ll need your full name, e-mail address, a password of 5 characters, your gender and at least one contact number.

HOW DO I BUY FROM TWEAR?

Find the product you wish to buY ,Add to cart ,Check out Choose your payment method and follow the steps to complete payment, then click on ‘Pay Now‘ to complete the order. A complete order summary will now display with all the details of your complete order.

WHAT ARE TWEAR.CO.ZA’S PAYMENT METHODS?

twear.co.za accepts a wide range of payment methods including Credit and Debit cards, Instant EFT, Cash On Delivery

WHEN DO I QUALIFY FOR COD?

Cash on delivery (COD) is offered as a payment option for certain orders, delivered within certain geographical areas, and on select products based on value. It will appear as an available payment option at checkout if your order qualifies.

WHAT ARE TWEAR.CO.ZA’S DELIVERY FEES?

twear.co.za offers a number of delivery options to choose from. Delivery is free for orders over R450.

WHAT COLLECTION OPTIONS ARE AVAILABLE?

twear.co.za offers collect specific Pickup Points.If a collection point is not close to your location, We will be able to deliver your package to your doorstep, anywhere in Johannesburg & Pretoria.

DOES TWEAR.CO.ZA HAVE THE SAME DELIVERY OPTIONS?

Yes, twear.co.za has most of the same convenient delivery options online clothing shops. twear.co.za also offers premium shipping services such as guaranteed same day delivery and weekend delivery options.

CAN I PAY WITH CASH?

When an item is marked ‘Eligible for Cash on Delivery’ on a product page, it means you can pay for this item with cash when your driver delivers your goods. Important: Currently we only accept cash payments when the Cash on Delivery option is selected. Credit & Debit Card or any other digital payments won’t be accepted on delivery.

WHEN WILL MY CREDIT CARD BE CHARGED?

Your credit card will be charged after the successful placing of your order and the approval of your card. If there are any problems, you’ll hear from us.

WHAT ARE MY DELIVERY OPTIONS?

Courier - We deliver directly to your home or office, anywhere in Johannesburg.All delivery and tracking details are supplied to you at checkout; Collect – You can choose to collect from Jeppestown

HOW DO I TRACK MY ORDERS?

Once we’ve received your order from our suppliers, we’ll send you an e-mail with tracking details. If in doubt, contact us.

WHAT HAPPENS IF A PRODUCT IS OUT OF STOCK?

If a product goes out of stock,it will reflect as such on the website or will no longer be visible on the website. If you have already ordered the product on the website when it goes out of stock, we’ll let you know. You will then have the option to be refunded or credited for any amount already paid by you.

IS IT SAFE TO SHOP WITH TWEAR?

We take the security of your payment and personal information seriously. See section 6 of our Terms and Conditions for details and the Payment FAQ below

CAN I PAY USING EFT?

Yes, Twear has two Instant EFT payment options via payment partners i-PAY and PayFast. Instant EFT is a payment service that allows you to pay via EFT without the hassle of a waiting period or additional fees.

HOW DO I CANCEL AN ORDER?

Get in touch with us here to cancel an item or a complete order. If the order has not yet been paid for, it can simply be cancelled. If it has been paid for, you have the option of a credit or a full refund.
